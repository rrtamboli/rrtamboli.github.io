Early Stage Researcher (01/2018 - 12/2018).

Holografika Kft., under the QoE-NET project

This position was funded from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation program under the Marie Sklodowska-Curie grant agreement No. 643072, Network QoE-Net.

During this term, I worked on assessment of perceptual quality of experience (subjective and objective) pertaining to projection-based light-field displays.
In particular, I explored the orientation preferences of 3D objects via user-interaction, viewing conditions of light-field video content, key performance indicators of light field visualization, light field compression, etc.
This research resulted in 13 co-authored publications, of which 2 are journal articles.
I also attended a few workshops, conferences and training sessions which took place within the European Union.

Intern

Yokohama Research Laboratory, Hitachi Ltd.

This internship was realized under the “Project for Future Researchers at Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad (IIT-H) to Enhance Network Development with Scholarship of Japan”.

This internship involved a pilot study on Compressive Sensing (CS) for Magnetic Resonance (MR) Imaging. Several existing sampling schemes and CS reconstruction algorithms were tested on real MR images.
